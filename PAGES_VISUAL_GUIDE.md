## Design System Consistency

All pages share these elements:

### Colors
- **Primary Yellow:** #FEC300 (buttons, highlights, backgrounds)
- **Secondary Yellow:** #F7BD15 (accents, dropdown arrows)
- **Dark:** #35363A (dark sections, text)
- **Black:** #222222 (main text)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (backgrounds, light text)

### Typography
- **Hero Headings:** 52px Product Sans Black
- **H1:** 48px Product Sans Bold
- **H2:** 28px Product Sans Bold
- **H3:** 24px Product Sans Bold
- **Body:** 15px Product Sans Regular

### Components
- Header (consistent across all pages)
- Navigation with active state
- Hero sections with background images
- Grid layouts (2, 3, or 4 columns)
- Card components with shadows
- CTA buttons in yellow
- Contact forms
- Footer (consistent across all pages)

### Spacing
- Section padding: 80px vertical
- Card gaps: 32px
- Content max-width: 1200px (6xl)
